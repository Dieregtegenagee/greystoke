Meet Coen Stokes

Your dedicated host with 3 years of hospitality experience

Coen Stokes is a passionate hospitality enthusiast committed to providing guests with exceptional self-catering accommodation. With over 3 years of proven hosting experience, Coen has built a reputation for quality, attention to detail, and genuine care for guest satisfaction.

At GreyStoke, we believe that hospitality is more than just providing a room—it's about creating a welcoming environment where guests feel at home. Every detail of our property, from the fully equipped kitchen to the secure parking, is thoughtfully designed with your comfort in mind.

Whether you're visiting Springs for business or leisure, we're here to make your stay memorable and comfortable.

Our Hospitality Philosophy

At GreyStoke, we're committed to delivering exceptional hosting experiences. Our philosophy is built on three core principles:

Quality & Attention to Detail

Every aspect of our property is maintained to the highest standards. From clean linens to fully stocked kitchens, we ensure you have everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Guest-Centric Service

Your satisfaction is our priority. We're responsive, communicative, and always ready to help make your stay better. We treat every guest with respect and genuine care.

Value for Money

We believe quality accommodation shouldn't break the bank. At R950 per night, you get access to premium amenities, central location, and dedicated hosting support.
