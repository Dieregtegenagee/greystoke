Room Layout

Master Bedroom

Spacious bedroom with comfortable double bed, fitted wardrobes, and en-suite bathroom featuring both bath and shower. Large windows provide natural light and ventilation.

Living Room

Comfortable living area with sleeper couch (sleeps 2), satellite TV, and access to fully equipped kitchen. Perfect for relaxation and entertainment.

Full Kitchen

Completely equipped kitchen with stove, oven, microwave, fridge/freezer, washing machine, and all necessary cookware and utensils for self-catering.

Outdoor Area

Secure parking and braai (BBQ) area available for guests. Perfect for outdoor entertaining and enjoying the Gauteng weather.
